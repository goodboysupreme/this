# Product

## Register

product

## Users

BITS Pilani students during placement, PS-1/PS-2, and SI season. Context: stressed, time-poor, often on mobile between classes or on a laptop at 2am. They come with one question ("can I get company X at my CGPA?") and want the answer in seconds, not a tour.

## Product Purpose

Placement & PS intelligence for BITS Pilani: historical CGPA cutoffs, stipend/CTC stats, branch eligibility, a statistical cutoff predictor, interview experiences, resume screener, JD match, and a cold-email centre. Success looks like: a student finds the number they came for without scrolling, and trusts it enough to act on it.

## Brand Personality

Precise, instrument-like, quietly confident. A data terminal built by a senior who has been through the process. Numbers are the hero; decoration is noise. Three words: precise, calm, earned.

## Anti-references

- The current design: indigo/violet gradients, glass cards, glow blobs, animated gradient text, noise overlays. The 2024 AI-SaaS default.
- Generic admin-dashboard kits: identical icon+heading+text card grids, gray-on-gray.
- EdTech brochure energy: cheerful illustrations, playful rounded everything, marketing fluff.

## Design Principles

1. The number is the hero. Cutoffs, stipends, and stats get the visual weight; everything else supports them.
2. One question per screen. Every page answers the specific question the student arrived with, above the fold.
3. Dense but calm. Real data density without visual anxiety: strong type hierarchy, real whitespace between sections, no decoration pretending to be information.
4. Never fake precision. The predictor is an estimate; the UI must say so in context, not in a footnote.
5. Speed is a feature. Fast loads, instant filters, no motion that delays reading a number.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, full keyboard access, visible focus rings, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected. Data tables stay readable on 360px screens (horizontal scroll with sticky first column, never truncated numbers).
